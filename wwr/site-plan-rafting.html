Purpose

Write a statement specifically identifying your target audience for a White Water Rafting site. Try to answer these questions: Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) What do they want? What are their needs that aren't being met? How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Add your Audience to your site plan just like you did for the Purpose above

Audience

Our target customers are adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts, typically aged 18-50, with an active lifestyle, who are looking for a thrilling white water rafting experience. They may be individual or groups of friends or families, They have moderate to high disposable income and are interested in outdoor activities, adventure and nature. They are looking for reliable, professional, and experienced rafting guides, as well as high-quality equipment and safety measures. They want to plan and book their rafting trips quickly and easily online. Their need is to find a reliable and convenient platform to book their rafting trips with all the necessary information in one place. They usually access our site through desktop, laptop, or mobile device
