Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide small business owners with a one-stop shop for creating and maintaining a professional online presence. The website will offer services such as website design and development, search engine optimization, and social media management, to help small businesses establish and grow their online presence.

Audience

I intend to create a website that provides small business owners with a one-stop shop for website design, search engine optimization, and social media management, to help them establish and grow their online presence. The intended audience is small business owners, individuals looking to start their own business, and freelancers or entrepreneurs in the field of online marketing.
